Welcome to Serene Touch

About Us

Serene Touch delivers personalized home care that honors independence while ensuring safety and comfort. We specialize in creating meaningful connections through compassionate assistance, companionship, and comprehensive support services. Our adaptive approach focuses on the whole person—physical, emotional, and social well-being—with care plans that evolve alongside changing needs. We maintain transparent communication with families, ensuring everyone feels informed and confident. Serene Touch is committed to enhancing quality of life while preserving dignity in every interaction.
Our Services

Our Vision

At Serene Touch Care Services, our vision is to become the trusted partner for compassionate care, serving as the go-to destination for both existing and new service users. We aspire to establish a reputation built on reliability, empathy, and exceptional service. Our commitment extends to fostering strong partnerships with our clients, ensuring that they not only receive quality care but also feel confident and secure in choosing Serene Touch Ltd as their dedicated provider. Through our unwavering dedication to compassion and reliability, we aim to be the preferred and dependable source of support for individuals seeking trusted caregiving services.
